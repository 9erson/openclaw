# Shekinah — The Cloud of God's Presence

## Hook (5 min — for everyone)

*Hold up thurible.*

**Ask:** What's this? When do we use it?

**Ask:** What happens when the smoke rises? What does it look like?

**Ask:** In the Bible, a cloud meant God was *here*. What do you think that means?

> **The cloud = God is here. That's the Shekinah.**

*Little ones walk away with: The smoke means God has arrived.*

---

## The Temple Cloud (2-3 min)

**Ask:** When the Ark was placed in the Temple, what filled the room?

> *"The cloud covered the tent of meeting, and the glory of the Lord filled the tabernacle. Moses could not enter because the cloud settled on it."* (Exodus 40:34-35)

**Ask:** What was that cloud called? *Shekinah* — "that which dwells."

**Ask:** So when the priest swings the thurible... what's entering the room?

---

## The Wilderness Pillar (2-3 min)

**Ask:** Before the Temple, how did God travel with Israel in the wilderness?

> *"The Lord went before them by day in a pillar of cloud to lead them along the way, and by night in a pillar of fire to give them light."* (Exodus 13:21-22)

**Ask:** What was the cloud doing? *Guiding them. God traveling with them.*

**Ask:** The incense smoke... what does it remind us of? *That pillar.*

---

## Not Just the Building (2 min)

**Ask:** After the Temple was destroyed, where did the rabbis say the Shekinah dwelt?

> *The Talmud says: "Whenever ten are gathered for prayer, there the Shekinah rests."* (Sanhedrin 39a)
> *"Wheresoever they were exiled, the Shekinah went with them."* (Megillah 29a)

**Ask:** So the cloud wasn't stuck in one building. Where was it? *With God's people, wherever they gathered.*

---

## The New Dwelling (2-3 min)

**Ask:** In the New Testament, where does God dwell now?

> *"The Word became flesh and dwelt among us."* (John 1:14)
> The Greek for "dwelt" means "pitched his tent" — same idea as the Tabernacle.

> *"Do you not know that you are God's temple and that God's Spirit dwells in you?"* (1 Corinthians 3:16)

> *"You are being built together into a dwelling place for God by the Spirit."* (Ephesians 2:22)

**Ask:** So when we gather for Mass... who is the Shekinah dwelling in? *Us. The Church.*

---

## Landing (1 min)

**Ask:** So when the incense rises at Mass, what are we seeing?

> *The cloud. God arriving. Dwelling with His people.*

> **The Shekinah is still here — in a new way — in us.**

---

## Teacher Notes

- Keep circling back: "So what does the smoke mean?"
- Let silence work. Rephrase if needed.
- End with: "Next time you see the thurible, remember — the cloud is here."
