# Acutis Flow

**Founded by:** Gerson Nazareth, Derick Dsouza

**What we do:** Help established businesses scale through AI-based technology solutions

---

## Target Audience
- Entrepreneurs and business owners with successful startups or businesses who want to scale but lack technical expertise
- Industry-agnostic - all industries served
- **No startups-from-scratch clients** (99% work with established businesses)

**Ideal client profile:** Existing business with proven model, wants to scale, recognizes tech gap, not technically inclined themselves. Example: Medi Guide (healthcare matching service in Pune).

## Services
- Websites, apps, marketing tools
- Technology solutions built using AI-driven coding
- Contractor model - we provide the "how", clients define the "why" and "what"

## Business Model
- Two co-founders, minimal overhead
- Leverage AI for fast, cost-effective development
- Commission-based salespeople for client acquisition
- Ongoing contracts for maintenance and infrastructure

## Non-Negotiables
- No partnerships requiring co-founder status or liability assumption
- No equity partnerships - contractor relationships only
- We build tech, not business strategy
- Exception: May rarely create own products (~1% of work)

## Success Signals
- Paid client acquisition (currently 3 tentative clients)
- Structured pricing model as we grow
- Better software, less time/resources
- Client retention and repeat business
- Expanding service offerings
- Future: potential hardware solutions

**Channel use:** Client management, project tracking, coordination
