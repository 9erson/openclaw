# Daily Brief Template
**Experiment:** 05:00 IST Consolidated Delivery
**Purpose:** Batch orchestrator questions into single digestible message

---

## 🌅 Morning Brief — [Day YYYY-MM-DD]

**3-5 key items across domains, delivered at 05:00 IST**

---

### Format

**Subject:** Morning Brief — [Day]

**Structure:**
1. **🔥 Priority** — 1-2 time-sensitive items needing decision/action
2. **📋 Status Checks** — 2-3 quick questions across domains
3. **📅 Today's Context** — Key events/deadlines

---

### Example Brief

```
🌅 Morning Brief — Wednesday, Mar 4

🔥 PRIORITY
• Opus Dei: Father Kevin's Consulate appointment (Mar 25, 9 AM) — is this booked yet?
• Bytebles: Webinar Part 1 is in 12 days — what's left on the prep checklist?

📋 QUICK CHECKS
• Santa Casa: Theme selected for March 11 mass prep?
• Acutis Flow: Medi Guide session Thursday — goal for this week's call?
• Knights: Valiants first event — have a date/activity in mind yet?

📅 TODAY
• Wed: Santa Casa classes at La De Estudiantes
• Thu: Bytebles Web Design class + Medi Guide session + Knights weekly (5:30 PM)

Reply to any item, skip the rest.
```

---

### Rules

1. **Max 5 items** — brevity is key
2. **One line per item** — quick to scan
3. **No repetition** — items mentioned once, not again unless urgent
4. **Urgent items starred** — 🔥 for same-week deadlines
5. **Time context** — always show what's happening today/tomorrow

---

### Success Metrics

- Response rate (any item answered = success)
- Gerson feedback (ask after 1 week)
- Brief vs. individual question comparison

_This template is experimental. Iterate based on response patterns._
